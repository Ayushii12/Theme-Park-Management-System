Celeste promises safety!

Read the following covid-19 guidelines thoroughly. In case of violation of any of the below mentioned measures the ones responsible will face consequences.

  1. Visitors will have to provide negative covid-19 report before entering the park.
  2. Everyone will have to wear masks even during the rides.
  3. Santitze your hands properly befor / after visiting the rides.
  4. Maintain six feet distance while standing in the queue.
  5. Avoid touching your eyes and nose.
  6. Do not shake hands with other visitors as well as staff memebers at celeste.
  7. Cover your mouth while coughing or sneezing.
  8. Do not throw used masks on the ground. Use trash cans for that purpose.
